About TEDxKIITUniversity

Welcome to TEDxKIITUniversity, where ideas worth spreading come to life. Our mission is to inspire and engage the community with thought-provoking talks and discussions from a diverse group of speakers.

Featured Speakers

Tom Thum

Topic: Beatbox brilliance

Bio: Armed with just a microphone, Thum pushes the limits of the human voice to create incredible soundtracks of impossible beats and phenomenal sounds, with scratched vinyl, the Michael Jackson back-catalogue, the didgeridoo and an entire fifties jazz band amongst his vocal repertoire.

Tim Urban

Topic: Inside the mind of a master procrastinator

Bio: Tim Urban knows that procrastination doesn't make sense, but he's never been able to shake his habit of waiting until the last minute to get things done. In this hilarious and insightful talk, Urban takes us on a journey through YouTube binges, Wikipedia rabbit holes and bouts of staring out the window -- and encourages us to think harder about what we're really procrastinating on, before we run out of time.
